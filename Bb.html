Gartner's Hybrid Model in Banking

  • **Buy** what you can: Off-the-shelf platforms fit standard, regulated processes like ledger, HR/payroll, KYC onboarding.
    Gartner: 60–75% needs covered [web:3]
  • **Build** for differentiation: Develop proprietary solutions (e.g. trading, wealth portals) for unique advantage; 10–15% of landscape [web:3][web:4].
  • **Blend** for integration: Use hybrid approaches to connect systems for compliance and agility [web:3].
  • Adopting this model can boost differentiation by **35%** while reducing costs—according to Gartner [web:3][web:7].

Why Banks Choose Hybrid

  • Rapid market entry via buying, with cost savings [web:8][web:10].
  • Innovation and customer experience via bespoke builds [web:4].
  • Seamless integration is a business enabler [web:3].
